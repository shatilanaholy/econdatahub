EconDataHub

Public Use Data Resources for Economics

EconDataHub catalogs over 100 distinct public-use data sources spanning microeconomics, macroeconomics, finance, and development. This repository prioritizes high-quality, specific datasets (such as longitudinal surveys and official censuses) over generic portals. It includes the full IPUMS suite, major US panel studies, and comparable international datasets like CHARLS, ELSA, and LASI.
